📲 Install on Android (Chrome)
1
Open this file in Chrome on your Android phone
2
Tap the ⋮ menu (3 dots, top right corner)
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
4
Tap Install — Spendly now lives on your home screen!
💡 Tip
If you don't see "Add to Home Screen", look for a small install icon (⊕) in the Chrome address bar.
🍎 Install on iPhone (Safari)
1
Open this file in Safari (not Chrome) on iPhone
2
Tap the Share button 📤 at the bottom
3
Scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap Add — done! 🎉
